>## Medieval English Theatre Meeting 29 March 2003 
> *New Directions in Medieval Theatre Research* 
> Lancaster University

**Map of Lancaster University**

The meeting will take place in **Furness SCR**. This is a one-storey building in the corner of Furness College main quadrangle (see map below). Entrance to the college is from the Spine, the central university walkway: go across the main foyer bearing right, and go into the quad through the glass door on the right. The SCR is immediately facing you.

**Parking**: there is free parking on Saturdays in the University. We recommend Library Avenue (see map below).
