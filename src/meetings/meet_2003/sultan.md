>## Medieval English Theatre Meeting 29 March 2003 
> *New Directions in Medieval Theatre Research* 
> Lancaster University


Dinner at the Sultan of Lancaster, 8.30 p.m. Friday 28th March

<img xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" src="/assets/koranic.jpg" style="margins:auto;display:block;width:;"/>

- The [Sultan of Lancaster](http://www.sultanoflancaster.com/) is in Brock Street, just over the traffic lights from Dalton Square and the Town Hall. See the map below.  

If you wish to chose your meal in advance, click on their [website](http://www.sultanoflancaster.com/) and you will find a menu (and examples of the interior decoration, with a friendly greeting from the proprietor Naim).  
You are advised that this is a Muslim establishment and therefore does not serve alcoholic liquor. The range of non-alcoholic drinks is however extensive and exciting.
