>## Saturday 24 March 2018
> **Performance and its Urban Context** 
> University of Sheffield

----

This year the Medieval English Theatre Meeting on *Performance and its Urban Context* will be hosted by Charlotte Steenbrugge at the University of Sheffield.

- [Programme](#programme)  
- [Registration](#registration)  
- [Hotels](#hotels)  

 

On the previous day (Friday 23 March) and in the same location there will be a **Postgraduate and Early Career
Symposium** run by the Early English Drama and Performance Network (registration free but space limited). **Speakers** include Michael Pidd (University of Sheffield), Toni Prince (University of Sheffield), Kia Macpherson (Hull Noah Play Project), Stuart Palmer (University of Kent), and Olivia Robinson (University of Fribourg). For details, programme, and registration [go to the EED&amp;PN website](https://earlyenglishdrama.wordpress.com/postgraduate-and-early-career-symposium/postgraduate-early-career-symposium-2018/) .
