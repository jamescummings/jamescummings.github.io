>## Saturday 27 March 2021
> **Environments** 
> **ONLINE** 
> Hosted by [Clare Egan](https://www.lancaster.ac.uk/arts-and-social-sciences/about-us/people/clare-egan) from the University of Lancaster.

 **The 2021 Medieval English Theatre Meeting** will take place **online** on **Saturday 27 March** between **9.30 a.m. and 6 p.m.** 

 [FREE **REGISTRATION** via Eventbrite **HERE** ](https://www.eventbrite.co.uk/e/medieval-english-theatre-online-conference-2021-environments-tickets-144246030761?aff=ebdssbonlinesearch).

- 9.30-9.35 **Registration**, log-in and sound-check   
- 9.35-9.50 **Welcome** (Clare Egan, Lancaster University)   
- 9.50-10.00 **Tribute to Peter Happé** (John McGavin &amp; Elsa Strietman)

 *Daisy Black*: ‘Being the bigger ram: Arable vs Pastoral Masculinities in the Towneley *Mactacio Abel* ’

- 11.30-12.30 **Session 2: Intercultural Environments**      
 *Nadia van Pelt*: ‘Perceptions of Anne of Cleves at the English Court’  
 *Elisabeth Dutton*: ‘Getting Down in the Haus of Holbein: where is Anne of Cleves now?’
- 12.30-13.30 **Lunch**    
- 13.30-15.00 **Session 3: Creaturely Environments** (Sponsored by the Northern Premodern Seminar)

 *Liz Oakley-Brown*: ‘Surfaces, Snails and Somatics in *Thersites* ’

 *Robert Crighton* (Beyond Shakespeare),

- 16.20-16.30 **Closing Remarks**    
- 17.00-18.00 **Business Meeting**
