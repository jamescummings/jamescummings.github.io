### Travel Instructions

 **Distances:** Southampton is roughly 3+ hours driving from Nottingham, 3.5 from Cambridge; six hours from Lancaster; 90 minutes from the S.W. London end of the M3 (in all cases add a bit for Friday travelling). We are aiming to meet at 6 in the Hotel and go to the restaurant for about 7 (to eat at 7.30). My mobile phone number, if you are in difficulties, or want to keep in touch about your progress is **07792 874 503** 

 **By train or by coach:** If you come by train, the simplest and cheapest thing is to get off at Southampton Central (not Parkway) and take a taxi from the rank outside to the �Elizabeth House Hotel, on the Avenue�. If you come by coach there is no taxi rank at the terminal. Just go into the coach terminal foyer and ask them to phone for a taxi. The taxi driver will then come in and get you.

 **By air (internal):** The quickest and best way, if you are near an airport serving Southampton. This will cut down an awful lot of driving and can be cheap. At Southampton airport get a taxi (you �book� them in the foyer) to �Southampton: Elizabeth House Hotel, on the Avenue�. I will return you to the airport for whatever flight you choose.

 **By car from the north:** From the north: take the M3 from London or, if coming from the Midlands via the A34 from Oxford, join the M3 at Winchester.  
- Go down M3  
- At junction 13 the M3 divides showing either M27 West or M27 East. It does not matter which you take, provided that you immediately afterwards  
- Leave the M3 at Junction 14 (signposted A33 Southampton). It�s now plain sailing.  
- Stay in the A33 lane (middle lane) and take the second exit at the roundabout (i.e., just carry straight on at the roundabout).  
-  (joining here) The Elizabeth House Hotel is on the left c.2.4 miles straight down the road from this roundabout.  
- You will go through another large roundabout (keep in the left hand lane and, in effect, just carry on the main road)  
- You will go straight over a major crossroads with lights  
- You will pass a large red brick church on your left (Avenue St Andrews)  
- When you pass this church the Elizabeth House Hotel is only a matter of a couple of hundred yards away, in the second block on the left.  
- Slow down; you are coming towards another major junction with lights and the Hotel is on the left before this junction, and just before the Travelodge on the corner of the junction.  
- There is plenty of parking.

 **By car from the east: ** You are on the M27 heading west  
- Leave the M27 at Junction 5 (Southampton Airport).  
- At the foot of the slip road turn left (i.e., take the first exit). You are on dual carriageway. Move into the right hand lane and   
- Take the first right (lights controlled) into Bassett Green Road.  
- Go up Bassett Green Road as far as you can until it swings round into a roundabout.  
- Take the first exit.  
- You are now in the same place as those coming down the M3; see (joining here) above and follow instructions from there.

 **By car from the west (check that the A36 through Salisbury is fully open if you are planning to take the scenic route)** You are on the M27 heading east  
- Leave the M27 at Junction 5 (Southampton Airport) and take the fourth exit (this will involve going round the roundabout and back under the motorway), heading for Southampton.  
- When you are on the dual carriageway, move into the right lane and  
- Take the first right (lights controlled) into Bassett Green Road.  
- Go up Bassett Green Road as far as you can until it swings round into a roundabout.  
- Take the first exit.  
- You are now in the same place as those coming down the M3; see (joining here) above and follow instructions from there.
